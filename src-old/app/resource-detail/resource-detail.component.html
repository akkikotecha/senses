FAQs

Images

1. Can I download product and project images?

You can download high-resolution images and project case studies in PDF format from our website.

2. Can I use and share the images commercially?

Unauthorized commercial use of our property is strictly prohibited. For more information, read our Terms of Use Policy.

3. The color of the actual product I received is different from that shown in the images, why is it so?

Please be aware that the PDF color shades can appear different from the real fabric colors. We advise you to request actual fabric samples before ordering.

Documents

1. Can I download brochures, specification sheets and assembly guidelines?

The Downloads Section is exclusively available for your convenience.

2. Can I order products of different dimensions than that mentioned in the Specification Sheet?

Customizations are not available unless specified on the product specification sheet.

3. Can you provide a video for product assembly guidelines?

While we don't offer video assembly services currently, we provide detailed and user-friendly installation manuals that make the process easy to follow.

4. I am having some doubts regarding the dimensions of the product, whom should I contact?

To discuss your exact requirements and address any specific inquiries, we recommend reaching out to our Sales Rep or Dealer in your location.

Materials

1. Can I order products other than the color mentioned in swatch cards?

While we strive to provide a wide range of color options, we cannot guarantee an exact match to specific expectations. However, we will do our best to find a color that closely aligns with your preferences from our extensive colorway options.

2. Why is there color variation in the swatch cards and the actual product?

Colors may appear slightly different in photos than in reality due to monitor variations.

3. Can I download CAD files?

Feel free to access and download the available 2D drawing files from our website.

Certifications

1. What is the authenticity of certificates and awards?

Our products are certified and awarded by renowned organizations. For more information on the certifications we hold, please visit our website.

2. Are the test reports validated by a Third party or valid institution?

Rest assured, our test reports undergo rigorous validation by esteemed institutions, ensuring their accuracy and reliability.
